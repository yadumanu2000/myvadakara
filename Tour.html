The municipality of Vatakara covers an area of 23.33 km2 (9.01 sq mi)[2] and is bordered by MahÃ© to the north and Payyoli to the south. It is the headquarters of Vatakara taluk, which consists of 22 panchayats. During the reign of the Kolathiris and Zamorins, Vatakara was known as Kadathanadu.
